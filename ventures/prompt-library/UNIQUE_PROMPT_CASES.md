# Unique Prompt Cases for Behavr

These are edge cases that generic/lesser models fumble. Each one is culturally specific, situationally nuanced, or otherwise non-obvious.

## Cultural Communication Styles

### Rejection / Saying No
1. **Rejection — Japan (Indirect/Face-Saving)** — The art of declining without saying no
2. **Rejection — Germany (Direct/Sachlich)** — Clear, reasoned, no fluff
3. **Rejection — India (Contextual/Relational)** — Maintaining relationships while declining
4. **Rejection — Indonesia (Saving Face/Collective)** — Never a flat no
5. **Rejection — Middle East/Gulf (Hospitable Decline)** — Warmth + door left open
6. **Rejection — Korea (Hierarchy-Aware)** — Different based on seniority
7. **Rejection — Brazil (Warm but Clear)** — Emotional intelligence + clarity
8. **Rejection — Nordic (Efficient/Egalitarian)** — Direct but not cold

### Cold Outreach by Region
9. **Cold Outreach — Japan (Keigo/Formal)** — Proper honorifics, indirect value prop
10. **Cold Outreach — Germany (Efficiency-First)** — Get to the point, cite credentials
11. **Cold Outreach — India (Relationship-Building)** — Establish connection before ask
12. **Cold Outreach — Middle East (Trust/Reputation)** — Referrals and mutual connections
13. **Cold Outreach — Southeast Asia (Face/Hierarchy)** — Appropriate positioning
14. **Cold Outreach — LatAm (Warmth/Personal)** — Personal touch matters
15. **Cold Outreach — Nordic (Egalitarian/Direct)** — Skip the hierarchy, be peer-to-peer

### Negotiation Styles
16. **Negotiation — Japan (Nemawashi)** — Building consensus before the meeting
17. **Negotiation — China (Guanxi)** — Relationship-based, long-term view
18. **Negotiation — Germany (Fact-Based)** — Data and logic win
19. **Negotiation — India (Flexible/Time)** — Patience and back-and-forth
20. **Negotiation — Middle East (Trust-First)** — Deal comes after relationship
21. **Negotiation — US (Direct/Win-Win)** — Get to terms quickly

### Support/Customer Service
22. **Upset Customer — Japan (Apology-First)** — Deep acknowledgment before resolution
23. **Upset Customer — Germany (Solution-Focused)** — Fix the problem, less emotion
24. **Upset Customer — India (Empathy + Escalation Path)** — Show you care + options
25. **Upset Customer — US (Empowerment)** — Make them feel in control
