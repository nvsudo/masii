So what's the catch?

There isn't one.

Masii makes money when you find your person — not when you keep searching. No subscriptions. No monthly fees. No paywalls between people who are meant to meet.

Traditional matchmaking platforms charge monthly subscriptions. That means their incentive is to keep you paying — show you enough promising leads to stay hopeful, not enough good ones to leave. The longer you stay, the more they earn.

Some charge on the first introduction. That creates a different problem — they need to show you enough leads to get you to sign up. The quality of the lead matters less than the volume.

A real matchmaker works differently. She gets paid when she finds you someone worth meeting. Not before. That's the model we followed. Masii charges per introduction — and only after both sides have seen the brief and said yes. If the right person shows up on introduction #1, that's all you ever pay.

Masii collects blessings, not subscriptions. 🪷

Questions people ask

What does "90%+ confidence" mean?

Masii evaluates dozens of signals — culture, values, lifestyle, family dynamics, dietary practices, life stage, geographic flexibility. When two people align strongly across the signals that matter most to both of them, that's a high-confidence match. Above 90%, the introduction is free.

What if the match is below 90%?

You still see the match brief with Masii's full reasoning — for free. If you're interested, you pay a per-introduction fee to exchange contact. You decide after reading the brief, not before.

Why does the woman decide first?

In traditional matchmaking, the girl's family has the first right. We modernized that. She sees the match first. She decides. Only if she says yes does he see it. This prevents spam, prevents unsolicited attention, and builds respect into the architecture.

What if I find my person on the first introduction?

Then that's all you pay. One introduction. Done. Masii isn't trying to sell you a second month. She's trying to get you to a wedding.

This is the point. We're not incentivized to keep you here.

How is "Masii on a Mission" different?

The base tiers match you within the people Masii has already spoken with. "On a Mission" means she goes looking — expands geography, searches adjacent communities, does multi-session deep profiling with you and your family. This is for harder searches: small community pools, global diaspora, very specific cultural requirements. It's the ₹50K traditional matchmaker experience, powered by AI.

Can my parents fill this out for me?

Yes. Parents can start the process. But Masii will always talk to you directly before any match is finalized. Both perspectives make better matches — but consent is non-negotiable.

Is my data safe?

Everything you tell Masii stays between you and her. Your answers are used only for matching — never displayed, never shared with your match or their family. No one browses your profile. Masii introduces, she doesn't display.

Why not just charge a subscription like everyone else?

Because subscriptions create the wrong incentive. A platform that charges monthly makes more money when you stay longer — which means they're incentivized to keep you searching, not to find you someone. We charge per outcome because we want the same thing you want: for you to find your person and go live your life.

Masii collects blessings, not subscriptions. 🪷
