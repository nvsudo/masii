Why your match should be free

Here's how matchmaking works in 2026: you create a profile. Someone likes you. You can't see who unless you pay. You pay. You see them. Maybe they're a good fit, maybe they're not. Either way, the platform got paid.

Shaadi.com charges for messaging. Hinge charges for seeing all your likes. Bumble charges for seeing who swiped right. The business model is the same everywhere: put a paywall between two people who want to meet.

We think that's backwards.

The Masii approach

Masii works differently. Our AI matchmaker looks at your profile and everyone else's in the community. When she finds a match with high confidence — two people who genuinely align on culture, values, lifestyle, and life stage — she makes the introduction. For free.

Both people see a summary of the match with Masii's reasoning. Both say yes. They connect. No paywall between them.

If the algorithm says you're right for each other, a paywall shouldn't stand between you.

Why this works

Trust. When users know the platform isn't withholding matches for money, they trust the process. They fill out more complete profiles. They engage more honestly. The data gets better, the matches get better.

Word of mouth. A successful free match creates a story. "Masii connected us — for free." In tight-knit diaspora communities, that story travels through WhatsApp groups, family dinners, and community events. One good match becomes ten referrals.

Better matching. When your business model doesn't depend on engagement, you can optimize for the right thing: quality matches. We'd rather show you one person with conviction than fifty profiles to scroll through.

So how does Masii make money?

We don't charge for the match. We charge for the extras that go beyond it.

Verified tier: Want to know that the person's income and photos are verified? That's a trust signal we can provide — for a fee. It costs us real resources to verify, and it's genuinely valuable.

Premium tier: Want Masii to do deep profiling — multi-session interviews, family context, active search across the community? That's concierge matchmaking. Like having a private matchmaker, but powered by AI and available to more people than the traditional $50K engagement.

The core match stays free. The value-add services are where we earn. This aligns our incentives with yours: we want to make great matches, because that's how we grow.

The philosophy

Every great relationship started with two people meeting. The moment of meeting shouldn't be monetized. It should be celebrated.

If you and someone else in this community are meant for each other — if the data says your values align, your lifestyles fit, your families would get along — you deserve to know. You deserve to meet. And Masii will make sure you do.
