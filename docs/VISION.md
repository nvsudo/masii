## The Problem

### The new generation is awake

More and more Indians — in India, abroad, everywhere — are waking up to what marriage means. What they want in a partner. What kind of life they want. They don't want the same lives as their parents. They want to choose.

But they also don't want to be cut off.

**The core tension:** Walking that fine line between choosing your life partner and matching your family vibe is a sandwich. You are getting independent but not cut off. You want agency over the biggest decision of your life — but you also want your parents to be happy, your culture to be respected, your values to carry forward.

No platform serves this person well.

### What exists today

**Shaadi.com / Matrimonial sites:** Built for the old model. Resume-style profiles. Parents browsing for children. Pay to unlock messages. The UX says "arranged marriage transaction." A growing number of Indians respect the model but reject the experience.

**Hinge / Bumble / Tinder:** Built for the West. Zero cultural context. Can't signal that vegetarian means no eggs, that gotra matters, that "close to family" means something specific. You match with people who look great on paper but have no cultural common ground.

**Traditional matchmakers:** The model actually works. Personal knowledge, cultural context, family dynamics — that's how great matches happen. But it's $5,000-$50,000 per engagement, limited by one person's network, and available only to the wealthy.

**The gap:** There's no product that says: "Choose your person. We'll help you find someone who fits your life AND your family. And we won't charge you for the introduction."

Masii fills that gap.

## The Solution

### Masii — Your AI Matchmaker

Masii (मासी — maternal aunt) is an autonomous AI matchmaker. She's the matchmaker auntie with a database brain, cultural fluency, and zero judgment. She understands that you want independence AND family compatibility — and she helps you find both.

**What Masii does:**
- Conducts intake conversations (WhatsApp, Telegram, or web form)
- Builds rich profiles through progressive disclosure (not 77 questions at once)
- Runs matching algorithms that weigh culture, values, lifestyle, and compatibility
- Presents matches with reasoning ("Here's why I think you two should meet")
- Manages double opt-in ("Both of you said yes — here's how to connect")
- Collects feedback after introductions to get smarter over time
- Publishes anonymized community signals to the website ("23 matches this month in Mumbai")
- Runs 24/7 without human intervention

**What Masii doesn't do:**
- Charge you to meet your match. If the algorithm says you're right for each other, the introduction is free.
- Replace human judgment entirely. Nik reviews edge cases. The premium tier brings in human matchmakers.
- Pretend to be human. Masii is AI, and proud of it. Transparency builds trust.

## The Insight: Free Matches Break the Mold

Every matchmaking platform today monetizes the introduction. Pay to see who liked you. Pay to unlock the match. Pay to send a message.

**Masii flips this.**

If the algorithm produces a high-confidence match (87%+), the introduction is free. Double opt-in. Both parties see a summary. Both say yes. They connect. No paywall between people who are meant to meet.

**Why this works:**
- Trust. Users believe in the platform because it's not withholding matches for money.
- Word of mouth. Free successful matches = organic growth in tight-knit communities.
- Better matching. When you're not optimizing for engagement, you optimize for quality.
- Revenue comes from value-add, not from hostage-taking.

**The free tier gives you:**
- Profile creation (form-based or conversational)
- Algorithmic matching on core compatibility
- Double opt-in introductions for high-confidence matches
- Basic profile (demographics, preferences, lifestyle)

**What free doesn't give you:**
- Verified income levels
- Verified photos
- Nuanced conversational understanding (AI-led deep profiling)
- Family-rounded view (parents' input, family dynamics)
- Active search (Masii working on your behalf)
- Date coaching
- Priority in match queue

## Revenue Model: Three Tiers

### Free — "You Deserve to Meet"
- Form-based intake (web or WhatsApp)
- Core matching algorithm (demographics + preferences + lifestyle)
- High-confidence matches (87%+) delivered free with double opt-in
- Basic profile: age, location, religion, education, preferences
- Self-service. Masii surfaces matches. You decide.

### Verified — "Trust, Confirmed" (~₹2,000-5,000/month or equivalent)
- Everything in Free, plus:
- Income verification (payslip/tax return upload, AI-verified)
- Photo verification (live selfie match)
- Enhanced matching (verified signals weighted higher in algorithm)
- Priority queue (verified profiles shown first)
- Badge on profile: "Income Verified" / "Photo Verified"

### Premium — "Masii Works For You" (~₹15,000-50,000/engagement or equivalent)
- Everything in Verified, plus:
- Deep conversational profiling (Masii conducts multi-session interviews)
- Family context (parents' input, family values, family dynamics)
- Active search (Masii proactively finds matches, not just algorithm)
- Human matchmaker consultation (uber model — freelance matchmakers on call)
- Date coaching (pre-date prep, post-date feedback)
- Revealed preference learning (what you say you want vs what you respond to)
- Concierge introductions (Masii facilitates the first conversation)
- Keeper.ai-level standards: thorough, evidence-based, high-touch

## Launch Strategy: Community Unlock

### The Cold Start Problem

Every marketplace has a chicken-and-egg problem. Dating platforms especially — an empty pool is worse than no pool. Rather than launching broadly and having a bad experience, Masii uses a **community unlock model**.

### How It Works

1. **Community/city signup:** People from a specific city or community (e.g., Gujarati professionals in Mumbai, Marathi community in Pune, Indians in Melbourne) sign up and create profiles
2. **Threshold:** When X profiles from that cohort join, matching unlocks for that group
3. **Expectation set:** Users know upfront: "We're building the [Community/City] pool. You'll be matched when we have enough people for quality matches."
4. **No bad experience:** Nobody waits in an empty room. They know the timeline. They can invite others to speed it up.

### Why This Works
- **Sets expectations** — users know this may take time
- **Creates urgency** — "invite 5 friends to unlock matching faster"
- **Ensures quality** — matching only starts when the pool is viable
- **Natural virality** — people recruit their own community
- **Community ownership** — "the Melbourne Gujarati pool" feels like theirs

### First Communities (To Be Decided)
Priority based on:
- Nik's network strength in that community
- Community density (enough people in one city/subgroup)
- Cultural preservation values (care about matching on culture)
- WhatsApp group penetration (easy to spread the word)

Expansion: community by community, city by city. Each community launch is a new unlock — same platform, tuned cultural context.

### Key Design Principles

1. **Autonomous first.** Nik doesn't run Masii. Masii runs herself. Human intervention is the exception, not the rule.

2. **Progressive disclosure.** Don't ask 77 questions upfront. Start light, deepen over time. Every interaction adds signal.

3. **Free matches = growth engine.** The best marketing is a successful match. Free intros create stories that spread through family WhatsApp groups and community networks.

4. **Cultural context is a feature.** Gotra matters. Diet matters. "Will you live with my parents?" matters. These aren't checkboxes — they're compatibility signals.

5. **Trust through transparency.** Show the numbers. Show the methodology. Show why Masii thinks two people should meet. No black box.

6. **Community unlock over empty launch.** Never launch to an empty pool. Build the community first, then unlock matching.
