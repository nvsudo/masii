How it works

Three conversations. One right person.

Masii asks you 36 questions — like the 36 gunas, but for real life. Your values, your lifestyle, your family, your future. Takes about 10 minutes. On Telegram, WhatsApp, or the web.

2

Masii finds your match

Masii searches your community for people who genuinely fit — culture, values, lifestyle, family vibe. She'll message you when she finds someone worth your time.

Masii shows you a match summary with her reasoning. If you're interested, the other person sees the same. Both say yes? Masii makes the introduction. It's free — because you deserve to meet.

Why Masii

Built for a generation that wants to choose — without being cut off.

Culture isn't a checkbox

Speaking Gujarati at home, valuing family dinners, knowing the difference between Jain and Hindu vegetarian — these things matter. Masii understands context, not just categories.

Your mom wants to help? She can fill out your profile. Your cousin wants to recommend you? They can. You want independence AND family harmony. Masii supports both. Consent is always required.

Most apps give you 50 profiles and say "good luck." Masii gives you one person and says "here's why." Quality over quantity. Conviction over volume.

Not a carousel

You're not shopping for a partner. You're finding one. Masii doesn't give you profiles to browse. She does the work, finds the person, and tells you why they fit your life and your family.
