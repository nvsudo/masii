01.

Everyone deserves a matchmaker

Traditional matchmakers charge ₹5 lakh to ₹50 lakh. That means the best matchmaking in the world is available to the wealthiest 1% of families. Masii believes what a great matchmaker does — listen deeply, understand context, find the right person — should be available to every Indian, everywhere. That's not charity. That's technology doing what it's supposed to do.

02.

A conversation, not a form

200-field forms capture data. Conversations capture people. When someone tells Masii they're "spiritual but not religious," she doesn't tick a box — she asks what that looks like on a Sunday morning. The follow-up question is everything. It's the difference between a database row and a human being.

03.

Independent but not cut off

This is the tension millions of Indians feel. They want to choose their partner. They also want their parents to be happy. These aren't contradictory — they're both valid. Masii is built for people navigating this space. Not arranged. Not "love marriage." Something that honours both.

04.

Culture is not a checkbox

"Hindu" on a profile tells you almost nothing. Masii knows the difference between a family that does puja every morning and one that goes to the mandir on Diwali. Between Jain-vegetarian-no-onion-no-garlic and vegetarian-but-eggs-are-fine. Between "close to family" meaning weekly video calls and "close to family" meaning joint household. Context, always context.

05.

She respects your secrets

There are things you'd never put on a Shaadi.com profile. Your real views on religion. Your past. The gap between what your family expects and what you actually want. Masii holds all of it without judgement. Your answers are used for matching, never displayed, never shared. The more honest you are, the better the match.

06.

One match, with conviction

Your grandmother's matchmaker didn't show up with a binder of 40 biodatas. She showed up with one name. And she could tell you exactly why. That's the model. Not a carousel. Not 50 profiles to swipe through. One person, with reasoning. If that match doesn't work, Masii goes back and thinks harder — she doesn't just pull the next name off a list.

07.

She decides first

Her side sees the match first. She decides. Only then does he see it. This isn't a feature — it's architecture. It prevents spamming, prevents unsolicited attention, and builds the kind of respect that should be the baseline, not the premium.

In traditional matchmaking, her family always had the first right. We kept what works.

08.

If you're meant to meet, nothing should stop you

When Masii's algorithm produces a high-confidence match — two people who genuinely fit on culture, values, lifestyle, and family — the introduction is free. No paywall between people who are supposed to meet. That's the philosophy. We make money from value-add services. The core match? That's sacred.

09.

Blessings, not subscriptions

Shaadi.com charges ₹500/month to keep you searching. Their incentive is to keep you paying. Masii charges per introduction — so her incentive is to find you the right person, fast. If the right match comes in week one, you pay once and go live your life. That's how a real matchmaker works. That's how Masii works.

10.

AI, and honest about it

Masii is not pretending to be human. She's a tool built to do one thing well: find your person. She learns from every conversation. She gets better over time. The goal isn't to replace human matchmakers — it's to make what they do available to everyone. Every community, every city, everywhere Indians are building their lives.

11.

Family is a feature, not a bug

Your mum wants to fill out your profile? Welcome. Your kaka wants to recommend someone? Great. Masii talks to everyone and triangulates — because the best matchmakers always did. But consent is non-negotiable. The actual person always gets the final word.

12.

The goal is to become unnecessary

The best thing Masii can say is goodbye. Not "here are ten more profiles" but "I think you two should talk, and I don't think you'll need me after this." No engagement metrics. No push notifications designed to pull you back. You tell Masii who you are. She finds your person. You go live your life together. That's the whole product.
