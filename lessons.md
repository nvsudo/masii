## Key Patterns (Updated Daily)

### What Works ✅
- Diaspora identity-first messaging (engagement spike when we own our identity)
- Vulnerability + real stories (personal struggles resonate more than marketing language)
- Community recognition (shoutouts drive loyalty + organic reach)
- Question-based formats (AMA, thread asks drive comments)
- Short video (15-30s) vs long (45s+)
- Diaspora-specific communities (niche beats broad)

### What Doesn't Work ❌
- Generic dating app language (gets scrolled past)
- Overly polished/corporate tone (feels inauthentic to diaspora)
- Long-form video on Instagram (underperforms vs short reels)
- Broad subreddit targeting (diluted audience, zero engagement)
- One-off posts without follow-up (no thread effect)

### Testing Ground
- Cross-platform hooks (what works on X might not work on Instagram)
- Time-of-day effects (posting at 10:00 vs 14:00 window)
- Video styles (testimonial vs narrative vs educational)
- Emoji usage (amount and type)
